OUR
JOURNEY

Since our inception in 2016, BrightBrain has transitioned from a niche agency specialising in real estate and manufacturing to a leader across various industries, including automotive, healthcare, hospitality, direct-to-consumer, education, and BFSI. We began with a vision to create an agency that focused on business outcomes deeply—not just through marketing but by being a part of strategic conversations at every level, not just at the CMO level, but at the boardroom, CEO and CFO level as well.


Our commitment to achieving remarkable business results has propelled our growth and enabled us to form prestigious partnerships. In 2019, our innovative strategies and consistent performance earned us recognition as a Google Elevator Partner, and by 2022, we were honoured as the Google International Growth Agency Partner. These accolades underscored our successful participation, highlighting the significance of our contribution.


Continuing our trajectory of recognition and success, 2024 was a landmark year as BrightBrain was chosen to be part of the prestigious Stanford Seed Transformation Program. This selection underscores our ongoing commitment to excellence in transforming business practices and driving digital success.
